Instructions

  1. To choose which bookmarks you want on the main page, search for them on the settings page by typing something and pressing enter or clicking search, right beside the search bar.
  2. When you have searched, click on any number of buttons to toggle their presence on the main page.
    • Green buttons are folders. Click to add the contents of that folder to the main page
    • Blue buttons are bookmarks. Click to add that bookmark to the main page
    • Grey buttons are bookmarks and folders that are already on the main page. Click to remove them from the main page
    • By clicking Remove All (next to Search), All bookmarks and folders get removed from the main page.
  3. On the main page, click the name of the bookmark you wish to change. It will change that bookmark to the url of your current tab.
